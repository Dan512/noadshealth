About BMI

BMI is a quick screening number, not a diagnosis. It doesn't distinguish muscle from fat, doesn't account for body composition, and isn't equally valid for everyone (athletes, older adults, and people of different ethnicities can fall outside its assumptions). Treat the result as a rough indicator and talk to a doctor or qualified trainer about what's actually right for your body.
