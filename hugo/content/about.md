**noadshealth** is a small, independent site that publishes honest, practical fitness advice. No display ads, no autoplaying video, no popups, no newsletter modals, no cookie banners, no analytics, no trackers, no affiliate banners — none of that.

## What you'll find here

Plain articles about workouts, nutrition, recovery, gear, and beginner-friendly programs, plus a few small browser-based calculators (BMI, with more on the way). Everything is meant to be useful first, monetized maybe.

## How the site stays free

Two things help cover costs, and that's it:

1. **Relevant Amazon affiliate links inside articles.** When an article recommends a specific piece of gear, the link to that gear on Amazon carries our affiliate tag. If you buy through it, Amazon pays us a small percentage — your price doesn't change. We only link to things we'd recommend anyway. As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases.
2. **Tips via [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/noadsdude).** The little "Support this site" button in the corner opens our Ko-fi page in a new tab. It's optional, the button can be hidden in Settings (gear icon, bottom-left), and clicking it doesn't track you — we can only see that you sent a tip if you actually send one.

That's the entire revenue model. There is no subscription tier, no paid content, no sponsored posts, no banner ads.

## Who runs this

noadshealth is part of the **NoAdsDude** family of small, ad-free sites. The same stance — *no ads, no tracking, no nonsense* — applies across all of them.

## A note on advice

Articles here are general-population guidance, not medical advice. If you have a condition, an injury, or you're pregnant or post-surgical, please talk to a qualified professional before changing what your body does.

## Questions

If something on the site is wrong, misleading, or out of date — please let us know. We'd rather fix it than leave bad info up.
